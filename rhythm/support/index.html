Pulse Rhythm Support

Welcome to Pulse Rhythm Support.

Pulse Rhythm is a rhythm training app designed to help users practice timing, tempo awareness, and musical precision.

If you need help using the app, have questions about subscriptions or purchases, or want to report a bug, please contact support .

We can help with:

  • app usage questions
  • bug reports and playback issues
  • subscription and purchase questions
  • feature requests and general feedback

When contacting support, it is helpful to include:

  • your device model
  • iOS version
  • app version
  • a short description of the issue

We review support requests as soon as possible and use your feedback to improve the app.

You can also review the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use in this app section.
